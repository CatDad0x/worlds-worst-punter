🐱 The Cat Dad Model — How It Works

The model runs in two steps.

Step 1 — Which team gets the first shot?

Uses match winner odds (70%) and each team's real attacking SOT rate from national stats (30%) to calculate the probability each team attacks first. Market-implied team strength is the primary signal — stronger teams generate the game's first meaningful attacking sequence more often than underdogs. Derived from the bookmaker's win/draw/loss prices using Poisson maths.

Step 2 — Which player on that team?

Once we know which team is likely to shoot first, we rank players within that team using these factors:

FactorWeightWhat it uses
📊 Bookmaker signal 42% William Hill/1xBet "Over 0.5 SOT" odds converted to a shot rate. Already prices in injuries, likely lineups & current form.
🌍 National team stats 18% Shots on target per 90 mins for their country — 60% weight on 2025/26, 25% on 2024/25, 10% on 2023/24. National role matters more than club role for this market.
🏟 Club form Blended 2025/26 club stats merged at 60% national / 40% club — because players shoot differently for country vs club.
⚡ Early-game timing 15% Shot-by-minute event data from 233 matches — Euro 2024, Copa America 2024, AFCON 2023, WC 2022. Tracks who shoots in the first 10 mins vs the 50th.
🎯 Position & role 15% Forwards/attackers +28% — they shoot earlier in games. Defenders −28%. Goalkeepers near zero.
🌐 Team attacking style 8% Team's aggregate national SOT rate. High-tempo teams (Spain, France, Brazil) get a boost; defensive sides get a discount.
🔵 Set piece takers 3% Known free kick specialists +7% — a direct FK in the first 5 mins is one of the most common early SOTs.
⚠️ Injury flag Dynamic Confirmed doubts get −65%. Bookmaker odds often lag on late fitness news — this is where we find edge.

📐 Reading the Edge

Edge = Cat Dad Model % − Bookmaker Implied %

The bookmaker implied % is calculated by normalising their "Over 0.5 SOT" odds across all players in the match. Our model % is our independent estimate. The gap is the edge.

🎰 How to Place This Bet

The market you're looking for is called "First Player to Have a Shot on Target" — not "Anytime Shot on Target". Here's where to find it on common bookmakers:

Bet365
Football → FIFA World Cup → [Match] → Player Bets → First Shot on Target
William Hill
Football → World Cup → [Match] → Player Specials → First Player to Have a Shot on Target
Paddy Power / Betfair
Football → World Cup → [Match] → Player Bets → First Player to Have a Shot on Target
Unibet
Football → FIFA World Cup → [Match] → Player Props → First Shot on Target

💡 The odds shown on this site (e.g. 1.32 for Bruno Fernandes) are from the Anytime Shot on Target market — we use those as input to build the model. The actual bet you place is the First SOT market, which will have longer odds. Always verify prices are live before placing.

⚠️ What This Model Doesn't Do (Yet)

  • Confirmed lineups — we don't yet factor in whether a player is starting. A high-probability player who's on the bench is worthless. Always check team news before betting.
  • Shot timing data — ✅ Now integrated from StatsBomb WC 2018 + 2022 event data. Players with real early-shot % data get a data-driven timing multiplier.
  • Team attacking style — ✅ Now integrated. High-tempo teams receive a boost in both Step 1 and the player model.
  • Opponent defensive profile — we don't yet adjust for position-specific concession rates (e.g. a team that gives up lots of early winger shots). Coming in v3.
  • Positive edge does not guarantee profit. This is a tool, not a guarantee.
